Dubai Schoggi

Introduction

This website is about making Dubai chocolate. I will explain step by step how it works!

Step 1

Materials & Tools:

  • Spoon
  • Knife
  • Braising pan and pot for the water bath

Ingredients:

  1. Chocolate
    • 2 bars of milk chocolate
    • 1 bar of white chocolate
  2. Kadayıf (fine angel hair pastry) – already prepared in the box
  3. Pistachio cream – in a jar

Step 2

In step 2, it's best to use a classic double boiler setup to melt the chocolate. Place a smaller pot inside a larger pot filled with almost boiling water and let the chocolate melt.

Step 3

Once the chocolate has melted, you can pour it into the mold and add a decoration of your choice.

Step 4

In the next step, melt the milk chocolate and pour it as the second layer into the mold.

Step 5

Now, cut the Kadayıf (fine angel hair pastry) into small pieces and mix them with the pistachio cream.

Step 6

The finished cream is then placed on top of the chocolate.

Step 7

You can melt the remaining chocolate and pour it over the cream at the end.

Step 8

Place the chocolate in the freezer for about 1–2 hours, and your product is ready.
